# Iran-US War 2026

Ongoing war with multiple dimensions:

- **Military**: Iran struck US bases in Middle East; satellite images show more damage than officially acknowledged (WaPo, CNN)
- **Hormuz blockade**: Iran closed the Strait of Hormuz (~20% of world oil); CIA assesses Iran can outlast the blockade for months
- **Undersea cables**: Iran proposes controlling all 7 internet cables through Hormuz — tolls, permits, Iranian-only maintenance
- **Internet shutdown**: 77+ days of internet blackout inside Iran
- **Economic**: Oil-price bets ahead of war news totalled $7B (Reuters); fertilizer shortages in US; energy price shock driving solar/heat pump demand in Europe
- **Cyber**: US exploited networking equipment backdoors (Cisco, Juniper, Fortinet, MikroTik) during strikes; Iran claims deep sabotage
- **Assessment**: The Atlantic — "Checkmate in Iran: Washington can't reverse or control the consequences of losing this war"
- **Ceasefire dynamics**: Israel intensified Lebanon strikes; Iran closed the strait in response
